About Andromida

History

Andromida ice skating has been around since April, 1957, creating and hosting a veriety of events for skaters of all ages to enjoy. At Andromida, our first priority is the safety and comfort of our skaters, so we are sure to keep our ice skating rink up to par with national and state standars, as well as providing good quality safety and skating geer to our customers and our employees that have to work on ice. Whats up with the Name?

Andromida Ice is named after the closest large galaxy to the Milkyway, "Andromeda". The Andromeda galaxy is in a uneque orbit with the Milkyway, and that is the experience we pursue to give here at Andromida, we aim to create a uneque environment for our skaters to enjoy with the help of ambient lighting and an exciting rotation of events for skaters to enjoy and take advantage of.
